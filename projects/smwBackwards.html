Super Mario World Backwards

This hack lets you play each level in the game backwards, inspired by Nathaniel Bandy's New Super Mario Bros. backwards videos.

This was made more so to be a "which levels are possible" kind of hack rather than a "here's a cool hack", so there's many levels that are impossible. Because of that, you can skip any level in the game if it's impossible or too hard.

If you want a hack that's more polished and meant to be possible, check out IsoFrieze's dlorW oiraM repuS.

WARNING: Choco Island 2 will softlock the game AND MAY CORRUPT YOUR SAVE, it's safer to skip that level.
